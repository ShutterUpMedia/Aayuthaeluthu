About us

உயிர் உள்ள உறவுகளுக்கு தொண்டாற்ற வருகிறது - "ஆயுதயெழுத்து"

Aayuthaeluthu is a non-governmental organisation founded in 2021 by a team of youngsters to contribute a bit towards societal improvements and to satisfy the needs of the society for its betterment. With Mr.Nandakumar IRS as chief mentor, we aim to remove the distress of people by lending them our helping hand wherever and whenever necessary.

Benefits of Volunteer

We provide a platform for the volunteers to understand society, serve society as well as improve themselves by delivering solutions for the problems in society. Our area's works are wider and we are making volunteers to gain experience in every possible field by allowing them to a new field every six months. We provide volunteers a better exposure and they would be able to develop their leadership qualities. Apart from societal works, we provide skill development sessions, career guidance sessions, especially for Aayudhaeluthu volunteers.

Benefits to the Society

We are working on various fields like Child & Youth, Environmental Care, Women's welfare, Social welfare, Pet care, Culture and Heritage to help the society in every possible way.Apart fron filed works, we are conducting awareness sessions for the benefit of public. Additionally, we are collecting funds for providing food and other needed stuffs for the betrayed and homeless. Also, we are encouraging volunteers to provide engineering solutions for the problems in their localities.

F.A.Q

Do you have any questions? Before asking, we would like to share some frequently asked questions to help you out!

  • Who can be the part of Aayutha Eluthu ?

    Anyone who has interest to serve for the good of the society can enroll yourself as a volunteer in Aayudha Eluthu.

  • Is there any membership fee ?

    No.Membership fee is not required to join our organisation.

  • What qualifications should I have to join the organisation ?

    We are expecting a highly active volunteering personality who must follow through commitments.

  • Will you give opportunities to volunteer to organise a whole event even when he/she has no postings?

    Yes. We will for sure. We always try to give opportunities and support to enthusiastic volunteers.

  • How can I join the organisation ?

    1. Fill out the application form : http://bit.ly/aayuthaeluthu 2. Our recruitment team will contact you shortly regarding further process.
